\doxysubsection{Detailed Description}
\doxylink{structnovaphy_1_1_joint}{Joint} descriptor connecting one link to its parent. 

Defines joint kinematics and motion subspace used by Featherstone forward/\+inverse dynamics. Axes are expressed in the joint-\/local frame.
