\doxysubsection{Detailed Description}
Spatial rigid transform between coordinate frames. 

Represents X\+\_\+\{A\texorpdfstring{$<$}{<}-\/B\} with rotation {\ttfamily \doxylink{structnovaphy_1_1_spatial_transform_adf9370a843aa6049798c28aaa5752d03}{E}} (B to A) and translation {\ttfamily \doxylink{structnovaphy_1_1_spatial_transform_ae53da5bc4b7e4961df7090cea0bcfd27}{r}} (origin of B expressed in A, in meters).
